Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long does shipping take for international orders?

International shipping typically takes 7-15 business days depending on your location. We provide tracking information once your order ships.

Are your products authentic traditional clothing?

Yes, all our products are handcrafted by skilled artisans from Balochistan and KPK using traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring genuine cultural representation.

What is Your return and Exchange Policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for items in original condition. Custom-made items may have different return terms. Please contact us for specific details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the courier's website directly.

Do you offer custom sizing or Alterations?

Yes, we offer custom sizing for most of our products. Please provide your measurements when placing an order, and our artisans will craft the item to your specifications for the perfect fit.
