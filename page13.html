Preserving Heritage

We bridge tradition with modern style, sharing Pakistan's cultural riches through authentic traditional clothing. Our pieces embody the spirit of Balochistan and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, crafted with heritage, passion, and timeless craftsmanship.

Our Story

Heritage Threads was born from a vision: to create a powerful platform to bridge the gap between overseas Pakistanis, communities and their rich cultural heritage. Founded in 2018, we recognized that many Pakistanis living abroad struggled to find authentic, high-quality traditional clothing with no nearby roots.

Our journey began when our founder, while living abroad, searched tirelessly for a traditional outfit for a family wedding. The available options were either mass-produced imitations or prohibitively expensive. This experience sparked the idea to create a business that would connect people directly with skilled artisans from their homeland.

Today, Heritage Threads proudly partners with over 50 cooperatives across rural Balochistan and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, ensuring that every piece we offer carries the authentic spirit of Pakistani craftsmanship. We believe that clothing is more than fabric: it's a connection to identity, culture, and the generations who came before us.

Our Mission Values:
